Which statement correctly describes the cancellation effect?
Correct
Response
Your
Response
Interference from magnetic fields created by electricity flowing through a copper wire can slow network communications. The cancellation effect pairs two wires with opposite signals in a circuit, and the magnetic fields cancel each other out. Coaxial cable, while it does use a copper conductor, does not have twisted wires. Fiber media do not use the cancellation effect because they do not use copper wires to transmit signals.?

A technician needs to add a new wireless device to a small WLAN. The WLAN is a mixture of old and newer 802.11b and 802.11g devices. What choice for the new device would provide the most interoperability for present and future growth?
Correct
Response
Your
Response
802.11n devices are interoperable with all the other standards, and provide more bandwidth than the other standards. 802.11a is not interoperable with any of the other standards. 802.11b and 802.11g interoperate with each other, but do not provide the bandwidth supplied by 802.11n devices.

Correct
Response
Your
Response
